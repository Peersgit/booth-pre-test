Stripe Connect has no monthly fixed fee — costs are purely transactional. This example reflects one crew member working an 8-hour shift at $50/hr. You collect the vendor total above, Stripe takes their cut, and that crew member receives their full $400.00. Fees scale per payout, so each crew member booked on a job runs through this same calculation independently.
$2.00 / active user / month
Stripe Connect also charges $2.00 per active connected account per month. An "active" account is any crew member who processes at least one transaction that month. At scale, this adds up — factor it into your per-crew pricing strategy.
