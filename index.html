$2.00 / active user / month
Stripe Connect also charges $2.00 per active connected account per month. An "active" account is any crew member who processes at least one transaction that month. At scale, this adds up — factor it into our per-crew pricing strategy.
1099's
In most states crew must make $600 to receive a 1099; Stripe handles them automatically but it comes at a cost:
  • $2.99 per 1099 e-filed with IRS
  • $1.49 per 1099 e-filed with states
  • $2.99 per 1099 mailed
